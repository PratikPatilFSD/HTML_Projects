Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. These systems can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, perception, and decision-making. AI encompasses a range of technologies that enable computers to perform these functions, including machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing.
